《Overloading》

Curation Art Work for NTU Art Fest
Its appearance resembles a recycling bin, encouraging audiences to "recycle" their abstract thoughts. Each pipe represents one of the four catagories, pain, knowledge, dream and memory.
To interact with this exhibit, you just have to write down your to-be-recycled thought, put it in one of the empty gacha balls we prepared, and put it in the correct pipe. Then, take one from the end of the pipe, and you will get to share someone else's recycled thought.

Due to the exhibit's high popularity throughout the art fest, we earned the opportunity to continue the exhibit at a well-known hostel, where our bin of beautiful thoughts connected more people with each other through interaction as such.
